Simple loop machine with OSC control.

\begin{tscattributes}
\attr{bpm} & Beats per minute (default: 120) \\
\attr{durationbeats} & Record duration in beats (default: 4)\\
\attr{ramplen} & Ramp length in seconds (default: 0.01)\\
\attr{gain} & Playback gain in dB (default: 0)\\
\attr{bypass} & Start in bypass mode (default: false)\\
\end{tscattributes}
