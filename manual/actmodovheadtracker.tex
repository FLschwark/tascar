headtracking module for MPU6050 from repository
\url{https://github.com/gisogrimm/ov-client}.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{name}       & Prefix in OSC control variables (default: ``ovheadtracker'')                                                                 \\
\indattr{devices}    & Space-separated list of potential serial devices (default: ``/dev/ttyUSB0 /dev/ttyUSB1 /dev/ttyUSB2'')                       \\
\indattr{url}        & Target URL for OSC data logging, or empty for no datalogging (default: ``'')                                                 \\
\indattr{ttl}        & Time-to-live of OSC multicast data (default: 1)                                                                              \\
\indattr{calib0path} & OSC-Path to which a trigger is sent on start of calibration path (default: ``/calib0'')                                      \\
\indattr{calib1path} & OSC-Path to which a trigger is sent on end of calibration path (default: ``/calib1'')                                        \\
\indattr{autoref}    & Filter coefficient for estimating reference orientation from average direction, or zero for no auto-referencing (default: 0) \\
\indattr{axes}       & Order of axes, or -1 to not use axis (default: ``0 1 2'')                                                                    \\
\indattr{actor}      & Pattern of \tascar{} object names (default: ``''). See actor modules for details.                                            \\
\indattr{accscale}   & Scaling factor of accelerometer (default: 16384/9.81, returns $m/s^2$).                                                        \\
\indattr{gscale}     & Scaling factor of gyroscope (default: 16.4, returns deg/s).                                                                  \\
\end{tscattributes}
