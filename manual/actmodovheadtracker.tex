headtracking module for MPU6050 from repository
\url{https://github.com/gisogrimm/ov-headtracker}.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{url}        & Target URL (default: osc.udp://localhost:9999/)                                   \\
\indattr{pattern}    & Pattern of \tascar{} object names (default: /*/*). See actor modules for details. \\
\indattr{ttl}        & Time to live of OSC multicast messages                                            \\
\indattr{devices}    & list of serial devices to test for headtracker                                    \\
\indattr{name}       & Name used for OSC variable prefix                                                 \\
\indattr{calib0path} & Path name to send to when calibration button is pressed.                          \\
\indattr{calib1path} & Path name to send to when calibration is completed.                               \\
\indattr{autoref}    & Auto-reference coefficient, 0 for no auto-referencing.                            \\
\end{tscattributes}
