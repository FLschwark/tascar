Create a window with a time display.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{times}     & List of marker times, or empty to use session time                            \\
\indattr{remaining} & Show remaining time until marker (true) or time since marker (false, default) \\
\indattr{fontscale} & Font scale (default: 1)                                                       \\
\indattr{threshold} & Change color to red if displayed time is below this value (default: 0)        \\
\indattr{x}, \indattr{y}, \indattr{w}, \indattr{h} & Position and size of window\\
\end{tscattributes}
