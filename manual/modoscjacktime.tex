This module sends the current jack time as OSC messages.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{url} & Destination URL; default: ``osc.udp://localhost:9999/''\\
\indattr{path} & Destination OSC path; default: ``/time''\\
\indattr{ttl} & Time to live of UDP messages; default: 1\\
\end{tscattributes}


Example:
\begin{lstlisting}[numbers=none]
<oscjacktime url="osc.udp://localhost:7000/" path="/time"/>
\end{lstlisting}
