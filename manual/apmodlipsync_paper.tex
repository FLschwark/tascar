Module to control lip synchronization as used in \cite{Llorach2016}.

\begin{tscattributes}
\attr{smoothing}      & Smoothing time constant in seconds (default: 0.04)                           \\
\attr{url}            & Target OSC URL                                                               \\
\attr{scale}          & Scaling factor of blend shapes; 3 values: kiss, jaw, lipsclosed              \\
                      & (default: ``1 1 1'')                                                         \\
\attr{vocalTract}     & Vocal tract scaling factor (default: 1)                                      \\
\attr{threshold}      & Noise threshold, range 0-1 (default: 0.5)                                    \\
\attr{maxspeechlevel} & Level normalization in dB (default: 48)                                      \\
\attr{dynamicrange}   & Mapped dynamic range in dB (defaul: 165)                                     \\
\attr{energypath}     & OSC destination for sending format energies, or empty for no energy messages \\
\attr{path}           & OSC destination of blendshape messages                                       \\
                      & (default: parent name)                                                       \\
\attr{sendmode}       & Sending mode, one of ``always'', ``transport'', or ``onchange''              \\
                      & (default: ``always'')                                                        \\
\attr{onchangecount}  & Maximum number of repetitions of equal messages in ``onchange'' mode         \\
                      & (default: 3)                                                                 \\
\end{tscattributes}
