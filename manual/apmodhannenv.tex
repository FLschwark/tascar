Apply periodic von-Hann ramps to the signal.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{t0}     & Start time in seconds                \\
\indattr{ramp1}  & First ramp length in seconds         \\
\indattr{steady} & Duration of steady state, in seconds \\
\indattr{ramp2}  & Second ramp length in seconds        \\
\indattr{period} & Period time in seconds               \\
\end{tscattributes}
