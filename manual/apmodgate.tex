Gate the vertex audio signal.

\begin{tscattributes}
\attr{tautrack}   & Min/max tracking time constant in seconds (default: 30)                          \\
\attr{taurms}     & RMS level estimation time constant in seconds (default: 0.005)                   \\
\attr{threshold}  & Threshold value between 0 and 1 (default: 0.125)                                 \\
\attr{holdlen}    & Time in seconds to keep output after level decay below threshold (default: 0.25) \\
\attr{fadeinlen}  & Duration of von-Hann fade in seconds (default: 0.01)                             \\
\attr{fadeoutlen} & Duration of von-Hann fade out in seconds (default: 0.125)                        \\
\attr{bypass}     & Bypass gate (default: true)                                                      \\
\end{tscattributes}
