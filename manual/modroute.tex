Create a jack bus with OSC controllable gain.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{name} & Jack and OSC identifier\\
\indattr{channels}& Number of channels; default: 1\\
\indattr{gain} & Gain in dB; default: 0\\
\indattr{lingain} & Linear gain; default: 1\\
\indattr{connect} & Regular expression of input port names\\
\indattr{connect\_out} & Regular expression of output port names\\
\indattr{mute} & Mute state (default: false)\\
\indattr{caliblevel} & Calibration value, used for metering (default: 93.98)\\
\indattr{levelmeter\_tc}     & Level meter time constant in seconds; default: 2                                     \\
\indattr{levelmeter\_weight} & Level meter weight type; default: Z (``Z'' or ``bandpass'')                         \\
\end{tscattributes}

The session OSC server is used for control.
