Speech activity and onset detector. This plugin creates an LSL outlet
and sends the states via OSC.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{tauenv} & Envelope tracking time constant in seconds\\
\indattr{tauonset} & Onset detection time constant in seconds\\
\indattr{threshold} & Envelope threshold in dB SPL\\
\indattr{url} & OSC destination URL\\
\indattr{path} & OSC destination path\\
\end{tscattributes}
