Very simple joystick motion controller module.  The recognized
attributes are:
\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{maxnorm}      & Maximum distance of object from origin, or zero
for no limit.                                                                       \\
\indattr{x\_ax}        & Axis number for control                                    \\
\indattr{x\_scale}     & Maximum velocity                                           \\
\indattr{x\_min}       & minimum value                                              \\
\indattr{x\_max}       & maximum value                                              \\
\indattr{x\_threshold} & Threshold for noise suppression                            \\
\indattr{preset}       & Preset selection, currently ``xbox360'' or ``logitechX3d'' \\
\indattr{device}       & Device name, or empty (default) for auto-detection         \\
\end{tscattributes}

{\tt x\_} can be replaced by {\tt x\_} (movement forward/backward), {\tt y\_} (lateral
movement), {\tt r\_} (rotation) or {\tt tilt\_} (tilt).
%
If a preset is selected and parameters set explicitly, then the preset
defaults will be overridden.
