\begin{tscattributes}
\attr{bpm} & Beats per minute (default: 120) \\
\attr{bpb} & Beats per bar (default: 4) \\
\attr{a1}, \attr{ao} & Amplitute of first and other beats \\
\attr{sync} & Use object time syncronization (default: false)\\
\attr{f1}, \attr{fo} & Resonance frequency of first and other beats\\
\attr{q1}, \attr{qo} & Filter resonance of first and other betas\\
\end{tscattributes}

OSC messages can be dispatched on beat one using the ``/dispatchin'' OSC variables.
