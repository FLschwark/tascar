Block loading of additional modules until specified jack ports exist.

\begin{tscattributes}
  \indattr{ports}   & Space separated list of jack ports to test                         \\
  \indattr{timeout} & Timeout in seconds after which execution will not block any longer (default: 30) \\
\end{tscattributes}

Ports can also be specified with \elem{port} sub-elements. This way it
is possible to include whitespace in port names, e.g.:

\begin{lstlisting}[numbers=none]
  <modules>
    <waitforjackport ports="obs:in_1 obs:in_2">
      <port>ardour:Giso/audio_in 1</port>
      <port>ardour:stereo/audio_in 1</port>
      <port>ardour:stereo/audio_in 2</port>
    </waitforjackport>
  </modules>
\end{lstlisting}
