\subsection{tracegui}\label{sec:tracegui}\index{tracegui}

A GUI module to show traces of a subset of objects, controlled by the
\attr{actor} attribute.

\begin{tscattributes}
\attr{tracelen}                     & Length of trace in seconds (default: 4)             \\
\attr{fps}                          & Display frame rate, frames per second (default: 10) \\
\attr{guiscale}                     & Zoom factor of GUI (default: 10)                    \\
\attr{unitcircle}                   & Show unit circle (default: true)                    \\
\attr{origin}                       & Show cross in origin (default: true)                \\
\attr{x},\attr{y},\attr{w},\attr{h} & Window position and size                            \\
\end{tscattributes}

An example which shows traces of all objects not starting with an ``o'':

\begin{lstlisting}[numbers=none]
<modules>
  <tracegui actor="/*/[!o]*" fps="20" guiscale="2.2" tracelen="1.6"/>
</modules>
\end{lstlisting}
