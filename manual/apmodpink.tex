Add a (band limited) pink noise to the input signal.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{fmin} & Minimum frequency in Hz (default: 62.5)\\
\indattr{fmax} & Maximum frequency in Hz (default: 4000)\\
\indattr{level} & RMS level in dB SPL\\
\indattr{period} & Period time of frozen noise in seconds (default: 4)\\
\indattr{use\_transport} & Play only if transport is running (default: false)\\
\end{tscattributes}

If \attr{use\_transport} is activated, the object time is used for the
frozen noise position.
