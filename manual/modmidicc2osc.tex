\subsection{midicc2osc}\label{sec:midicc2osc}\index{midicc2osc}

Convert MIDI CC events into OSC messages.

\begin{tscattributes}
\indattr{url} & OSC destination URL\\
\indattr{path} & OSC path\\
\indattr{dumpmsg} & Dump unprocessed messages to console\\
\indattr{name} & name of MIDI client\\
\indattr{connect} & name of input MIDI source\\
\indattr{controllers} & List of controllers, in ``channel/param'' form (e.g., 0/13 0/28) \\
\indattr{min} & minimum output value (corresponding to MIDI 0)\\
\indattr{max} & maximum output value (corresponding to MIDI 127)\\
\end{tscattributes}
